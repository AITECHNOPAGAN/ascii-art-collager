FAQ

Q: What is this RPG about?

A: This is a collaborative roleplay experience based on Sailor Moon, where players take on the roles of Sailor Senshi and embark on adventures together.

Q: Do I need experience to join?

A: No! Both beginners and experienced roleplayers are welcome. We're here to have fun together.

Q: What is WBS?

A: WBS is the chatting system we use for real-time roleplay. You'll need an account to participate.

Q: Can I play multiple characters?

A: Contact the GMs to discuss your interest in playing multiple characters.

Q: What happens if I miss a session?

A: We understand life happens! Just let the GMs know if you'll be absent. You can miss up to three meetings before we have to have a conversation.

Q: Why can't I play as either Eternal Sailor Moon or Super Sailor Uranus?

A: These are the GM's characters. Part of the advantage of being the GM is that you can get the character you want. Of course, Usagi and Haruka work hard to keep our game working and running.
